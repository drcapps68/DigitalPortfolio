Daniel Capps

I am fascinated with new technologies and methodologies. In addition to being a Certified Scrum Master, I have 15+ years of managerial leadership experience in dangerous, high-stress situations and with multi-million-dollar businesses. With a Bachelor's degree in Software Development along with practical knowledge and experience leading high-profile, high-pressure software releases, I have the experience, skills and results-driven attitude to succeed in engineering software which are mobile-first, modern and up-to-date.

I am a military Veteran. While my Secret Security clearance is no longer active, it has helped me better understand the rigors of the several Non-Disclosure Agreements required for the proprietary technologies I have worked with while being a system analyst and a web developer.

I excel in team dynamics and truly enjoy working with others. I appreciate great managers and team leaders and strive to be who my team needs in whatever role I am placed. This team mindset, in conjunction with my leadership capabilities and my love of Agile methodologies, helped me to successfully lead numerous teams, including a multi-national team.
